Welcome to Call of Duty Veteruns - the first in-game charity race within Call of Duty®: Warzone™ Pacific! 

Join the Call of Duty Endowment by lacing up your boots to celebrate placing 100,000 veterans into high-quality jobs. For every 10km you run in game between May 26 – June 3, Activision Blizzard will allocate $1 as part of a $1 million total donation to the Endowment (up to $10/player) to place the next 100,000 veterans.

 

Opt-in below to share your progress on the Call of Duty® Veteruns Leaderboard where we will display the top 100 runners in the world.

 

Remember to stay frosty and take plenty of breaks. After all, who said running for a good cause couldn’t be fun?
